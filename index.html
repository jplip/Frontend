Key for Rarities

If the card has no glow on the outside it is a Common Card.

If the card has an Orange Glow then it is a Rare Card.

If the card has a Purple Glow then you have received an epic card.

If your card is in a Hexagon Shape it is a Legendary Card.

If your card has a Gold Outline then it is a Champion Card.
